About Us

Small-Batch,
Big Intent

ZAISH was born in the studio, not a boardroom. We believe denim should be more than just a commodity—it should be a craft.

Founded on the principles of transparency and sustainability, we combine traditional denim construction with modern design sensibilities. Every piece is part of a small-batch run, ensuring quality and reducing waste.

Beyond our collections, we open our doors to universities and teams through workshops, sharing the art of upcycling and the reality of fashion sustainability.
